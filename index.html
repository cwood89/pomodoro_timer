What is Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro technique was created by Francesco Cirillo as a way to get more done in less time. If used properly you will instantly see results in your productivity.

There are 6 steps in the Pomodoro Technique:

  1. Choose a task.
  2. Set a timer for 25 minutes.
  3. Work in the task until the timer rings.
  4. When the timer rings, put a check mark on paper.
  5. Take a 5 min break.
  6. Every 4 cycles of this take a longer 20 to 30 minute break.
